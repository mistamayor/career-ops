## C) Nivel y Estrategia

### Nivel detectado vs candidato
- **JD level:** Head of / IT Director-adjacent, in a company going through a transformation phase, with team leadership and hands-on delivery.
- **Candidate natural level:** Head of IT / IT Director / Technology Director. Profile fits the upper end of "Head of Technology" (£4M budget, 20k endpoints, 49 countries prior scope) — this Lincoln role is actually *smaller scope* than his last Bolloré role.

### Plan "sell senior without lying"
- Lead with **scale + outcome metrics**, not job title:
  - "Directed £4M IT budget across 49 countries, delivered 22% under budget"
  - "Migrated 24+ mission-critical apps VMware → Azure/VCF at 99.95% uptime"
  - "Stabilised a failing Dynamics 365 BC programme: −20% timelines, +20% adoption, −15% dev costs"
- Frame recent **consulting phase (Jan 2025 – present)** as a deliberate bridge: Telefónica Tech for NHS/HCPC/LINX + WyzPay + Kerakoll — "modern stack, multi-client delivery discipline."
- Use "hybrid infrastructure" as a direct callback — on-prem + Azure is Mayowa's default operating environment.

### Plan "if I get downlevelled on comp"
This is the real risk, not title. £75-80k is below target.
- **If the role scope matches (team size, budget, transformation mandate):** negotiate **£85-95k + review at 6 months tied to measurable transformation milestones** (service desk SLA improvement, cloud migration %, ISO27001 audit pass, Copilot AI rollout).
- **Ask for written promotion/review criteria** before accepting. "What are the criteria for a salary review at 6 or 12 months?"
- **Benefits/RSUs/bonus:** if base is fixed, push for car allowance / bonus / pension above statutory / private health — Peaple often negotiates package, not just base.
- **If they cannot move past £80k and scope is Head-of-IT-sized:** politely pass. Baseline UK Head of IT sits £85-95k (Ashdown/Robert Half mid-band) and candidate's current market stand is £100k+.
- **Contract alternative:** if permanent comp is the blocker, ask whether they'd consider a 6-12 month contract first (£550-650/day = £130-155k annualised), converting to permanent with upgraded comp after proving value.
